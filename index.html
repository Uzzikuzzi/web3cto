FAQs

You will receive my expertise as a highly skilled and experienced CTO, working hands-on on your product to deliver the highest quality iterations and developments possible.

Working with an agency may involve long communication cycles and unclear budgets. Most of the time you will work with junior folks and PMs. Our subscription model offers a CTO led approach and more flexibility and transparency.

Finding a good developer is hard and usually you need two devs at least to cover backend and frontend. Hiring a developer will not provide the same level of expertise and hands-on involvement as my CTO-led approach.

Freelancers can be unreliable and may not have the same level of expertise as me. Our subscription model offers commitment from myself.

In case there is a need, I will work with other developers to fill in the gaps and ship faster. These are trusted professionals with relevant expertise. How fast will I receive my software using plug and play plan? I will put maximum effort to get your idea to production as soon as possible. Usually, it takes 2-3 months to see an initial version.
